About Me

My creative process is finding something that I find interesting and something that I think I will enjoy working on. I also enjoy learning new techniques and being challenged with complex creations. I enjoy more simple designs that are not overwhelming but are still interesting. I also think that a simple design is hard because I just like to keep adding things so creating simple designs is a bit of a challenge for me as I have to stop at some point.

I enjoy designing as I constantly create things in many different ways. This can include a 3D model in blender or using Unity to make games. I also have done web design which is a combination of design and programming which I enjoy. I hope to be able to have my job be around design as I enjoy creating things and problem solving / fixing stuff
